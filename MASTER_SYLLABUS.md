# Master Syllabus: Missionary Readiness Institute

## Overview
The *Missionary Readiness Institute* platform provides comprehensive, visually rich training for independent missionaries, church members, mission sponsors, and small organizations. All courses include structured lessons, visual frameworks, curated reading lists, and field-tested templates.

**Status as of 2026-06-08:** Micro-phase complete. All 28 lessons across 11 courses include visual frameworks, reading lists, and operational guidance.

---

## Level 1: Foundational Starter Courses
Entry-level courses for those considering or beginning cross-cultural service.

3. Humility, Accountability, and Support

5. Next-Step Readiness Conversation

**Field Tools (to generate):**
