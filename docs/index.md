# Missionary Readiness Institute

Practical training for faithful cross-cultural service.

Missionary Readiness Institute helps churches, sending teams, and prospective workers prepare for the spiritual, cultural, relational, and practical realities of missionary service. The platform is designed as a public training library now, with room to grow into a fuller course experience over time.

## Training Focus

Our readiness material emphasizes:

- Spiritual formation for life and ministry under pressure.
- Cross-cultural humility, listening, and learning.
- Healthy teamwork, communication, and missionary care.
- Practical preparation for language learning, field entry, and service rhythms.
- Wise expectations for churches, mentors, and sending partners.
