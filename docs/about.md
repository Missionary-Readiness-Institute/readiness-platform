# About

Missionary Readiness Institute exists to help future cross-cultural workers prepare with clarity, humility, and courage.

The site is built for learners who want grounded preparation before entering missionary service, and for churches or mentors who need a simple way to guide conversations about readiness. The aim is not to replace local discipleship, pastoral care, or sending structures. Instead, the platform gives those relationships a shared training framework.

## Who This Is For

- Prospective missionaries discerning next steps.
- Churches and sending teams preparing workers.
- Mentors, coaches, and trainers looking for discussion-ready material.
- Learners who need introductory guidance before deeper field-specific training.

## What We Emphasize

Readiness is more than information. It includes spiritual maturity, cultural humility, relational health, teachability, practical planning, and dependence on God. The course structure is designed to support reflection, conversation, and action rather than quick completion alone.

## Current Status

This repository currently hosts the public website and starter course structure. Content should be treated as draft educational material until reviewed and adopted by the appropriate organization or ministry leadership.
