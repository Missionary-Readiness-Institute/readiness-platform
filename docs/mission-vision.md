# Mission & Vision

## Mission

Missionary Readiness Institute provides practical, faithful, and accessible training resources that help cross-cultural workers prepare for sustainable missionary service.

## Vision

We envision churches and sending communities equipped to prepare workers with wisdom before they enter the field. Readiness should be spiritual, relational, cultural, and practical, helping servants arrive as learners who can love well, communicate clearly, and endure faithfully.

## Guiding Commitments

- **Faithful formation:** Readiness begins with worship, Scripture, prayer, obedience, and character.
- **Cross-cultural humility:** Workers enter new contexts as learners, not experts.
- **Whole-person care:** Durable service requires attention to spiritual, emotional, relational, and practical health.
- **Church partnership:** Training should strengthen, not bypass, local church discernment and sending.
- **Practical application:** Every course should help learners prepare for real conversations, decisions, and field conditions.

## Training Outcomes

Learners should be able to name their next preparation steps, identify risks and support needs, communicate with sending partners, and approach cross-cultural service with teachable confidence.
