Missionary Readiness Institute currently operates this site as a static GitHub Pages / MkDocs Material website. The site does not currently provide user accounts, directly collect course registrations, or directly process payments.

## Information We Collect

This site is primarily informational. We may receive limited information if visitors contact us directly, submit feedback through a linked external tool, or interact with third-party services used to host or measure the site.

Possible information may include:

- Name, email address, and message content if a visitor provides it voluntarily.
- Basic technical information handled by GitHub Pages or other hosting services, such as IP address, browser type, device information, and access logs.
- Analytics information if analytics tools are added later.
- Information submitted through external tools, such as Google Forms or email, if those tools are linked from the site.

## Sensitive Information

Do not submit sensitive personal information through general contact forms, public links, or email addresses listed on this site. This includes medical, counseling, legal, immigration, tax, financial, child protection, security, or crisis information.

## How We Use Information

Information may be used to:

- Respond to inquiries.
- Improve site content and usability.
- Maintain site security and reliability.
- Communicate about training resources or partnerships when requested.

## What We Do Not Do

We do not intend to sell visitor personal information. We do not intend to collect sensitive pastoral, medical, legal, immigration, child protection, tax, counseling, financial, or security information through this public site.

We do not currently create user accounts directly on this site or handle payment processing directly through this site.

## Third-Party Services

The site is currently hosted through GitHub Pages. GitHub and other third-party providers may process technical data according to their own policies.

External tools may be used later for forms, email, analytics, embedded media, course communication, or certificates of completion. If those tools are added, visitors should review the privacy practices of those services, and this policy should be updated as needed.

## Data Retention

Messages and technical records should be retained only as long as needed for ministry, operational, security, or legal purposes.

Questions about this policy may be sent to `contact@example.org`.
