# Reentry Debrief Guide

## Purpose
A 7-question debriefing guide for returning workers to reflect on their field experience and transition home.

## Guide questions

1. What was the most significant lesson from this assignment?
2. What was your biggest challenge returning home?
3. What support did you need most during reentry?
4. What unresolved tensions should be addressed now?
5. How did your understanding of mission or calling shift?
6. What relationships do you need to maintain or restore?
7. What practical next steps do you need to take in the next 90 days?

## Notes

- Use this guide in a debrief conversation with a mentor or care team.
- Record insights and follow-up actions.
- Treat the debrief as part of ongoing worker care.
