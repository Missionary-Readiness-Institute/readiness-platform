# The RAFT Transition Checklist

## Purpose
A step-by-step worksheet for planning Reconciliation, Affirmation, Farewells, and Thinking Destination before leaving a host culture.

## How to use this checklist

1. List relationships or issues requiring reconciliation.
2. Identify people who helped you and plan how to affirm them.
3. Plan intentional farewells with key contacts.
4. Think through the practical and emotional destination after departure.

## Notes

- Reconciliation may include apologies, closures, or practical repairs.
- Affirmation can be verbal, written, or expressed through service.
- Farewells should be intentional and culturally appropriate.
- Thinking destination helps set realistic expectations for the next phase.
