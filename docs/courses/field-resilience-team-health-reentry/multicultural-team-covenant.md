# Multicultural Team Covenant

## Purpose
A tool for diverse teams to establish baseline rules for conflict management and communication before entering the field.

## How to use this covenant

1. Discuss communication expectations as a team.
2. Agree on conflict management practices.
3. Record commitments for mutual care and accountability.
