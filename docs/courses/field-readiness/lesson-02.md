# Lesson 02: Environmental Awareness and Threat Levels

## Key Idea

Field readiness depends on correctly assessing the environment so you can respond appropriately and avoid unnecessary risk.

## Lesson Goal

Understand how threat levels shape field preparation and evacuation thinking.

## What This Lesson Teaches

- Field readiness depends on correctly assessing the environment so you can respond appropriately and avoid unnecessary risk.
- How the idea connects to the course output.
- A practical action you can complete immediately.

## Practical Action

Describe whether your current destination is low, medium, or high threat and what that means for your planning.
