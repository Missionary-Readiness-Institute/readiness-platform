# Lesson 04: Documents, Logistics, and Support Contacts

## Key Idea

A field-ready plan includes passports, visas, embassy contacts, health records, and trusted local partners.

## Lesson Goal

Prepare the necessary documents and local support contacts before departure.

## What This Lesson Teaches

- A field-ready plan includes passports, visas, embassy contacts, health records, and trusted local partners.
- How the idea connects to the course output.
- A practical action you can complete immediately.

## Practical Action

Create a simple list of three critical documents and three support contacts you must have before traveling.
