# Lesson 04: Narrative Learning and Storying

## Key Idea

Storying is a field-ready way to share truth in cultures that learn through narrative, proverbs, and drama.

## Lesson Goal

Practice an oral, narrative method that works well in non-literate cultures.

## What This Lesson Teaches

- Storying is a field-ready way to share truth in cultures that learn through narrative, proverbs, and drama.
- How the idea connects to the course output.
- A practical action you can complete immediately.

## Practical Action

Choose one Bible story and practice telling it without notes, focusing on the core message and simple questions.
