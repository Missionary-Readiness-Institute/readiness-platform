# Lesson 05: Next-Step Readiness Conversation

## Key Idea

A readiness conversation should be specific, honest, and tied to observable preparation steps.

## Lesson Goal

Prepare a focused conversation with a mentor, pastor, sponsor, or sending partner.

## What This Lesson Teaches

- A readiness conversation should be specific, honest, and tied to observable preparation steps.
- How the idea connects to the course output.
- A practical action you can complete immediately.

## Practical Action

Draft three conversation prompts for a readiness meeting and set one follow-up action after the meeting.
