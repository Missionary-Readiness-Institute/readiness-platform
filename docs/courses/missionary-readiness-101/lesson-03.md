# Lesson 03: Humility, Accountability, and Support

## Key Idea

Independent service should not mean isolation. Healthy readiness depends on people who can speak truth, pray, and care for the worker.

## Lesson Goal

Recognize the relationships that make field service sustainable and avoid isolated decision-making.

## What This Lesson Teaches

- Independent service should not mean isolation. Healthy readiness depends on people who can speak truth, pray, and care for the worker.
