# The Three Buckets Worksheet

## Purpose
Provide a simple field tool for sorting local cultural practices and beliefs into the Top Bucket, Bottom Bucket, and Middle Bucket.

## How to use this worksheet

1. Identify one local practice, belief, or custom.
2. Ask whether it is required, prohibited, or optional in the local context.
3. Use the worksheet columns to record the meaning and your gospel response.

## Notes

- Top Bucket items are seen as required for identity, survival, or belonging.
- Bottom Bucket items are viewed as harmful or prohibited.
- Middle Bucket items are optional, neutral, or culturally different without moral weight.
