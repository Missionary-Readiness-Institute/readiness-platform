# Illustration Filter Matrix

## Purpose
A practical tool to evaluate whether a teaching story or illustration is appropriate for the local community.

## How to use this matrix

1. List the story or illustration you plan to use.
2. Identify whether it fits Category 1 through Category 5.
3. Choose the clearest and most locally relevant story available.

## Categories

- **Category 1:** Foreign concepts nobody understands
- **Category 2:** Foreign concepts some may understand
- **Category 3:** Familiar concepts with foreign values
- **Category 4:** Locally experienced and understood
- **Category 5:** Experienced but not understood; useful as a bridge

## Guidance

- Prefer Category 4 whenever possible.
- Use Category 5 only when it connects with shared experience and points to Gospel truth.
- Avoid Category 1 illustrations that depend on foreign institutions or assumptions.

## Next step
