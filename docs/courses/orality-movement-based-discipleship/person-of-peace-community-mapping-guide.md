# Person of Peace Community Mapping Guide

## Purpose
Help tentmakers and field workers map local networks and identify potential Persons of Peace in their workplace or neighborhood.

## How to use this guide

1. List your daily contacts through work, church, market, and social life.
2. Note who is open, who listens, and who influences others.
3. Identify the people most likely to welcome the Gospel and open their network.

## Questions to ask

- Who shows interest in spiritual matters?
- Who invites conversation or offers hospitality?
- Who others trust and follow?

## Notes

- Tentmakers often find Persons of Peace through ordinary work relationships.
- Respect each contact and avoid manipulating relationships.
- Pray for guidance before approaching potential Persons of Peace.
