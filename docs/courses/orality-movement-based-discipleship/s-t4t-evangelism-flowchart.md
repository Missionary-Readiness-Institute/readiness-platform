# S-T4T Evangelism Flowchart

## Purpose
A step-by-step guide for moving from listening to sharing the Possessed Man story and the Creation to Church panorama in S-T4T evangelism.

## Process

1. Listen to their cultural story.
2. Tell Your Story (personal testimony).
3. Tell a Hook story (e.g., the Possessed Man in Mark 5). Ask: "Do you want to be changed like this person?"
4. If yes, tell the Creation to Church story.
5. Ask for commitment and next steps.

## Flowchart

| Step | Action | Key question |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | Listen to their story | What does this story say about their world? |
| 2 | Tell Your Story | How has Jesus changed you? |
| 3 | Tell Hook story | Do you want to be changed like him/her? |
| 4 | Tell Creation to Church | Will you follow Jesus now? |
| 5 | Follow-up | Who will walk with you? |

## Notes

- Keep each step short and oral.
- Make the hook story simple, vivid, and locally relevant.
- Use the Creation to Church story as the Gospel climax.
