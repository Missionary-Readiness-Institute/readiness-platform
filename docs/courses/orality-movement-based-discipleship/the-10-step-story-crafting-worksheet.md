# The 10-Step Story Crafting Worksheet

## Purpose
A field tool for crafting a Bible story that is culturally relevant, narrative-driven, and ready for oral delivery.

## How to use this worksheet

1. Identify the principle or Gospel truth.
2. Evaluate worldview bridges and barriers.
3. Select a story that fits the local context.
4. Craft the story in simple, oral language.
5. Communicate the story as an intact narrative.
6. Apply the story through dialogue.
7. Invite obedience based on the story.
8. Build accountability into the follow-up.
9. Reproduce the story with others.
10. Review and adapt for the next listener.
