# Gift Acceptance Policy

## Purpose

A framework defining how restricted and unrestricted gifts are processed, approved, and documented.

## Policy statement

Our ministry accepts gifts responsibly, honoring donor intent and protecting organizational integrity.

## Gift categories

- Unrestricted gifts: funds that may be used for general ministry purposes.
- Restricted gifts: funds designated for a specific project, activity, or timeframe.
- Conditional gifts: funds given with conditions that must be accepted or declined.

## Donor intent

Donor intent must be captured in writing for every restricted or large gift. If intent is unclear, the ministry will seek clarification before acceptance.

## Approval process

- Gifts under $____ may be accepted by the field leader.
- Restricted or large gifts require approval from: ___________________________
- The gift acceptance committee or board must review and document the decision.

## Documentation

- Record donor name, amount, gift purpose, and date.
- Attach donor instructions and any written clarifications.
- Store records for at least ___ years according to local legal requirements.

## Use of funds

Restricted gifts will be used only for the designated purpose unless the donor agrees to a change.

## Review

This policy will be reviewed annually or whenever gift handling procedures change.

## Notes

- Review this policy with legal and financial advisors.
- Do not accept a gift that conflicts with the ministry’s values or mission.
