# Knowledge Check

This course uses a graded quiz delivered through Google Forms after finishing the lessons.

- Question count: 5
- Passing threshold: 4 correct responses
- Format: multiple choice and short answer
- Link: [Complete the graded course quiz](https://forms.gle/REPLACE_WITH_YOUR_FORM_LINK)

## Quiz Focus

- Biblical foundations for support raising
- Partnership mapping and the God Ask
- Ethical storytelling and media consent
- Donor retention and pastoral care

## Preparation

- Review the lesson skill checks.
- Complete the practical output: donor calendar and consent policy.
- Discuss your drafts with a mentor or support partner.
