# 90-Day Language Acquisition Plan (LAP)

## Purpose

A modular schedule that combines TPR, Picture Book review, and community language projects over 90 days.

## Instructions

Fill in each weekly plan, choose one LAP project per month, and review progress with a mentor or helper.

## Plan overview

- Month 1: Build listening, power tools, and basic commands.
- Month 2: Deepen vocabulary with picture books and shared experiences.
- Month 3: Execute language projects and strengthen emotional stamina.

## Monthly LAPs

- Month 1 LAP: A guided local conversation, market purchase, or greeting exchange.
- Month 2 LAP: A shared activity with a community member, such as cooking or working together.
- Month 3 LAP: A more complex task, such as leading a short prayer, teaching a phrase, or visiting a local service.

## Notes

- Adjust the plan to your context and pace.
- Share the plan with a sending team or mentor for accountability.
- Include rest days to prevent burnout.
