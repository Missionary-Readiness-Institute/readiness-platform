## Purpose of the agreement

This agreement describes how the helper will support the learner in developing language skills through regular sessions and careful correction.

## Helper responsibilities

- Meet with the learner as agreed, ideally 3–5 times per week.
- Use the target language as much as possible.
- Help the learner with pronunciation, repetition, and correction.
- Explain simple vocabulary, gestures, and cultural norms.
- Respect the learner’s dignity and emotional state.

## Learner responsibilities

- Arrive on time and ready to practice.
- Use power tool phrases to keep the session in the target language.
- Review new words between sessions.
- Be respectful of the helper’s time and service.
- Share how the helper can improve the session.
