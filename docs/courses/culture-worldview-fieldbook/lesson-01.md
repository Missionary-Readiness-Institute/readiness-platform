
# Lesson 01: The Six Worldview Lenses

## Key Idea

Field practitioners map culture by looking past surface customs and identifying the worldview assumptions that shape them.

## Lesson Goal

Learn the six foundational lenses that reveal how a society understands reality.

## What This Lesson Teaches

- Players, Core Foci, Tools, System, Theme, and Goal.
- How each lens reveals a different part of local meaning.
- Why field practitioners must observe more than customs.

## Practical Action

Choose one local custom and note what you can observe for each worldview lens.
