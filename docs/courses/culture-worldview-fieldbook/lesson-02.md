
# Lesson 02: Understanding Animism and Avoiding Syncretism

## Key Idea

A clear field practice must separate Christian grace from animistic merit and manipulation.

## Lesson Goal

Understand how animistic assumptions can produce syncretism if believers are not taught a biblical worldview.

## What This Lesson Teaches

- Why syncretism is often a mix of Christian and incompatible worldview assumptions.
- How animism treats spirits as manipulable forces.
- Why the gospel must be explained as relationship with a wise, loving God rather than a magic system.

## Practical Action

Write one short paragraph describing how you would explain to a local learner that God is not a spirit to be bribed, but a loving Creator who calls for relationship.
