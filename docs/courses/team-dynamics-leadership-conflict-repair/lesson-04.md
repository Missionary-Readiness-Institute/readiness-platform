# Lesson 04: The Huddle Principle and Shared Burdens

## Key Idea

Healthy teams share hardship and create regular rhythms for debriefing and support.

## Lesson Goal

Build practical structures for shared burden, peer support, and burnout prevention.

## What This Lesson Teaches

- Why teams should not leave members isolated with hard work.
- The value of regular huddles, debriefs, and shared accountability.
- How rotating burden and care can keep the team resilient.

## Practical Action

Create a simple huddle plan with frequency, participants, and one shared accountability question.
