# Lesson 01: Decentralized Leadership

## Key Idea

Healthy teams share authority and responsibility rather than concentrating power in one leader.

## Lesson Goal

Learn the starfish leadership model and when decentralized decision-making is stronger than central control.

## What This Lesson Teaches

- The benefits of distributed leadership for resilience and local ownership.
- How teams can organize around shared purpose rather than a single leader.
- Why trust, communication, and clarity are essential for decentralized teams.

## Practical Action

List three ways your team could share leadership responsibilities more evenly.
