# Lesson 03: Shifting Perspectives in Conflict

## Key Idea

Conflict often grows when people are seen as obstacles rather than individuals with needs.

## Lesson Goal

Use the Arbinger Institute framework to shift from self-focused thinking to people-centered care.

## What This Lesson Teaches

- How self-deception changes the way team members see each other.
- Why conflict repair begins with mindset, not only behavior.
- Practical steps to restore trust and move from blame to responsibility.

## Practical Action

Write one example of a team conflict and how you could reframe it by seeing the other person as a person, not a problem.
