# Knowledge Check

This course uses a graded quiz delivered through Google Forms after finishing the lessons.

- Question count: 5
- Passing threshold: 4 correct responses
- Format: multiple choice and short answer
- Link: [Complete the graded course quiz](https://forms.gle/REPLACE_WITH_YOUR_FORM_LINK)

## Quiz Focus

- Decentralized leadership and team resilience
- High-stakes dialogue principles
- Shifting perspectives in conflict
- Shared burden and team huddle structures

## Preparation

- Review the lesson skill checks.
- Complete the Team Covenant and Conflict Repair Plan.
- Discuss your drafts with a teammate or mentor.
