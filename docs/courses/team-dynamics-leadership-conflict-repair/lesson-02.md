# Lesson 02: Navigating High-Stakes Dialogue

## Key Idea

Difficult conversations are inevitable. The key is to keep them safe, honest, and focused on mutual purpose.

## Lesson Goal

Apply principles from *Crucial Conversations* to bring clarity to stressful team interactions.

## What This Lesson Teaches

- How to notice when a conversation has become high-stakes.
- The importance of shared purpose and mutual respect.
- Practical habits for speaking honestly without damaging relationships.
