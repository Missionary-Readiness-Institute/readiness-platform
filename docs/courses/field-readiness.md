# Field Readiness

## Purpose

Help learners prepare for practical field entry with clear expectations, wise planning, and healthy communication.

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this course, learners should be able to:

- Identify practical questions that should be addressed before departure.
- Prepare communication rhythms with sending partners.
- Recognize basic risk, logistics, and transition concerns.
- Clarify next steps with church, agency, or team leaders.

## Starter Modules

1. Field entry expectations.
2. Documents, logistics, and transition planning.
3. Communication with supporters and sending churches.
4. Risk awareness and wise escalation.
5. First-year learning goals.

## Reflection Prompt

What practical uncertainty is most likely to create avoidable stress, and who can help you address it?

!!! caution
    This course does not provide legal, immigration, security, or medical advice. Consult qualified professionals and responsible leaders for field-specific decisions.
