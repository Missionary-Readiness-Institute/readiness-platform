# Lesson 03: Local Helpers and Feedback

## Key Idea

A local helper can offer correction, cultural insight, and encouragement in ways a textbook cannot.

## Lesson Goal

Use local helpers to accelerate learning and avoid embarrassing errors.

## What This Lesson Teaches

- A local helper can offer correction, cultural insight, and encouragement in ways a textbook cannot.
- How the idea connects to the course output.
- A practical action you can complete immediately.

## Practical Action

Identify one local helper or language partner and agree on a simple feedback routine.
