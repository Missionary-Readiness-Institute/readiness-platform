# Lesson 04: Managing Frustration and Progress

## Key Idea

Language learning can feel slow. Real progress comes from small wins and persistence, not perfection.

## Lesson Goal

Build realistic expectations and celebrate small progress.

## What This Lesson Teaches

- Language learning can feel slow. Real progress comes from small wins and persistence, not perfection.
- How the idea connects to the course output.
- A practical action you can complete immediately.

## Practical Action

Note one frustration you expect and one small progress marker you will celebrate each week.
