
# Knowledge Check

This course uses a graded quiz delivered through Google Forms after finishing the lessons.

- Question count: 6
- Passing threshold: 4 correct responses
- Format: multiple choice and short answer
- Link: [Complete the graded course quiz](https://forms.gle/REPLACE_WITH_YOUR_FORM_LINK)

## Required Assessment

Download and complete the **Worldview & Tool Swap Assessment** before or after the quiz.

- Link: [Worldview & Tool Swap Assessment](worldview-tool-swap-assessment.md)
- Focus: map one Christian practice and explain how a local worldview might misinterpret it.
- Outcome: a short written reflection that can be reviewed with a mentor.

## Quiz Focus

- Worldview basics and the three cultural buckets
- Syncretism versus dual allegiance
- The first and second circle behavior model
- Tool swaps and player confusion
- Replacing cultural core focus with gospel focus

## Preparation

- Review the lesson skill checks.
- Complete the practical assignment and the assessment.
- Discuss your reflections with a mentor or local leader.
