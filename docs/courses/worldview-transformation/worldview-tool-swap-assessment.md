Use this assessment as a downloadable course resource. Complete it with a mentor or field leader and save it as part of your practical output.

## Instructions

1. Review the Worldview Transformation course lessons.
2. Choose one Christian practice or ritual, such as prayer, communion, tithing, spiritual leadership, or storytelling.

## Part 4: Gospel Clarification

- What is the biblical purpose of the practice?
- How can you teach the difference between a Christian tool and a local spiritual tool?

## Part 5: Reflection

- What is the most important difference between the Christian practice and the local spiritual tool?
- What is one action you can take to keep the gospel distinct while still honoring local culture?
