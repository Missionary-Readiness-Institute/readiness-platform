# Knowledge Check

This course uses a graded quiz delivered through Google Forms after finishing the lessons.

- Question count: 5
- Passing threshold: 4 correct responses
- Format: multiple choice and short answer
- Link: [Complete the graded course quiz](https://forms.gle/REPLACE_WITH_YOUR_FORM_LINK)

## Quiz Focus

- 501(c)(3) basics and board governance
- Gift acceptance and donor intent
- Field accounting and petty cash controls
- Local NGO registration and international identity

## Preparation

- Review the lesson skill checks.
- Complete the practical output templates.
- Discuss your governance plans with a mentor or advisor.
