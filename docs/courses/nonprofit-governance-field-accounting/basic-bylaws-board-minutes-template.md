# Basic Bylaws & Board Minutes Template

## Purpose

A starter structure for small nonprofits to document governance and board decisions.

## Bylaws outline

1. Name and purpose of the organization
2. Mission and mission boundaries
3. Board composition and terms
4. Officers and responsibilities
5. Meeting frequency and quorum
6. Decision-making authority
7. Conflict-of-interest policy
8. Amendment process

## Notes

- Keep minutes concise but clear.
- Record motions, approvals, and any conflicts of interest.
- Store minutes with official records.
