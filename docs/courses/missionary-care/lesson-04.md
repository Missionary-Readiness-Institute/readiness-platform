# Lesson 04: Support Communication and Recovery

## Key Idea

Communication with sponsors, mentors, and local leaders should include honest reports of strain and clear recovery steps.

## Lesson Goal

Plan a support rhythm that helps workers recover from difficulty.

## What This Lesson Teaches

- Communication with sponsors, mentors, and local leaders should include honest reports of strain and clear recovery steps.
- How the idea connects to the course output.
- A practical action you can complete immediately.

## Practical Action

Draft a short support update format that includes wins, struggles, and next steps.
