# Lesson 05: Sustainable Care Habits

## Key Idea

Sustainability is not a one-time fix. It is a consistent practice of rest, accountability, and mutual care.

## Lesson Goal

Commit to care habits that are realistic for long-term field service.

## What This Lesson Teaches

- Sustainability is not a one-time fix. It is a consistent practice of rest, accountability, and mutual care.
- How the idea connects to the course output.
- A practical action you can complete immediately.

## Practical Action

Choose one weekly care habit and one accountability partner to review it with you regularly.
