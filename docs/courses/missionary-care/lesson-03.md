# Lesson 03: Boundaries, Energy, and Rest

## Key Idea

Field workers must learn when to say yes and when to preserve time, energy, and emotional bandwidth.

## Lesson Goal

Create healthier boundaries and energy habits for sustainable service.

## What This Lesson Teaches
