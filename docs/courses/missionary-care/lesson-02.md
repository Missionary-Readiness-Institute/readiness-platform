# Lesson 02: Team and Family Health

## Key Idea

Good missionary care looks beyond individual productivity to the health of the whole team and any family members involved.

## Lesson Goal

See how field care includes both team and family wellbeing.

## What This Lesson Teaches

- Good missionary care looks beyond individual productivity to the health of the whole team and any family members involved.
- How the idea connects to the course output.
- A practical action you can complete immediately.

## Practical Action

List one support habit for yourself and one for your team or family, then name a partner who can help you keep it.
