# Privacy Policy

Effective date: `To be added`

This privacy policy is a starter template for Missionary Readiness Institute's public website. It should be reviewed and completed by the responsible organization before public launch.

## Information We Collect

This public MkDocs site is primarily informational. It may collect limited information if visitors contact us directly, submit feedback, or interact with third-party services used to host or measure the site.

Possible information may include:

- Name, email address, and message content if provided voluntarily.
- Basic technical information handled by hosting providers, such as IP address, browser type, device information, and access logs.
- Analytics information if analytics tools are later enabled.

## How We Use Information

Information may be used to:

- Respond to inquiries.
- Improve site content and usability.
- Maintain site security and reliability.
- Communicate about training resources or partnerships when requested.

## What We Do Not Do

We do not intend to sell visitor personal information. We do not intend to collect sensitive pastoral, medical, legal, immigration, or security information through this public site.

## Third-Party Services

The site may be hosted through GitHub Pages or another public hosting provider. Those providers may process technical data according to their own policies. If analytics, embedded media, forms, or email services are added later, this policy should be updated.

## Data Retention

Messages and technical records should be retained only as long as needed for ministry, operational, security, or legal purposes.

## Contact

Questions about this policy may be sent to `contact@example.org`.
